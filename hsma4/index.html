Si Chun’s Health Service Modelling Associates (HSMA4) Project Pitches

The following videos set out my four project pitches as part of the HSMA 4 programme run by PenCHORD at the NIHR ARC South West Peninsula. The four proposals represent the diverse areas of work undertaken by local authorities, from public health, to housing and homelessness, and arts and culture; and each use a different operational research modelling approach, namely, discrete event simulation, system dynamics, agent-based simulation, as well as machine learning for sentiment analysis. Video captions are also provided.

Si Chun Lam
December 2021

 

  • Using discrete event simulation to determine the effectiveness of introducing community spirometry to reduce pressures on the healthcare system

     

  • Creating a system dynamics model of the feedback loops resulting in ‘super stranded’ long-staying hospital patients

     

  • Using agent-based simulation to model the factors influencing the supply and demand for social housing

     

  • Using sentiment analysis to understand audience and participant satisfaction at Coventry 2021 the UK City of Culture
